Rock The Tundra coming October 25th to a Tundra near you!

Hey, everyone! It’s an honor to get to tell you that we are hosting Rock the Tundra on October 25th this year! It will take place out on the Tundra at ONU from 11 AM - 9 PM! Come enjoy some local Ohio bands & see their visions come to life! We’ll have fun games and great food to pass the time!

But wait... what is Rock the Tundra?

This year marks the 5th anniversary of our famous charity concert. Started by proud alumni James Goslee, Jake Colwell and Michael Janiak, this event has been our biggest charity event since it’s inception in 2021. Over the past 4 years we have raised around $6,000 for the USO & Music Will through our amazing blend of live music, food, fun games and cool merch. This past Rock the Tundra was our most successful yet, raising a whopping $2122 thanks to the wonderful people who came to support us and the mountain of sponsors who helped us make this a reality.

We have many bands making their return to the big stage. Oracion is returning with their indie and shoegaze and the Wheely Brothers with their Country Blues. We hope to see others return! We also want some new bands to arrive, one of which being Black Phosphorus, a rock band who has done some good gigs in Ada.

When can I join the fun?

Our event was sadly pushed back to October 25th, but we believe that this will be a great thing for the event. By having more time to set it up, we can perfect our plan and make this a wonderful, smooth day for everybody.

So please keep your eyes peeled for updates about this year’s “Rock the Tundra!” We have been working hard to put together a fun time and we are excited to see how it all comes together! We hope you can join us and have a great time! See you all there!
