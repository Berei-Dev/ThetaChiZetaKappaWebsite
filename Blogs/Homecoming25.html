Homecoming begins on October 3rd!

Hey, everyone! For those of you who come down every year for homecoming, Ohio Northern will be hosting homecoming on October 3rd! Ohio Northern University invites Polar Bears back to campus to celebrate Homecoming and Reunion Weekend! Friday and Saturday are filled with plenty of awesome events for you to enjoy and get to reunite with your college friends!
